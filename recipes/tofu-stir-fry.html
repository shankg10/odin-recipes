Tofu Stir Fry

Description

Whip up a tasty tofu stir-fry with veggies in a flash! Just toss tofu and veggies in a hot pan with your favorite sauce for a quick, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Firm Tofu
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Preparing the Ingredients:
    • Gather your ingredients: firm tofu, mixed vegetables, soy sauce, cooking oil, and garlic.
    • Cut the tofu into cubes and chop the mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
    • Mince the garlic.
  2. Cooking the tofu-stir-fry:
    • Add minced garlic to the pan and saut√© until fragrant.
    • Add the tofu cubes to the pan and cook until golden brown on all sides.
    • Next, add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender but still crisp.
    • Pour soy sauce over the tofu and vegetables, stirring well to coat evenly.
    • Continue cooking for a few more minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  3. Serving and Enjoying:
    • Once the tofu stir fry is cooked, turn off the heat
    • Serve the tofu stir fry hot, garnished with optional toppings like green onions or sesame seeds.
    • Enjoy your delicious homemade tofu stir fry!
